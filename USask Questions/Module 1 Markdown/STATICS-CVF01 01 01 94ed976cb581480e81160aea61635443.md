# Feedback:

a. This is the top view of the x-y plane, and the third axis, the z-axis, should come out of the page. Recall the right-hand rule: if we take our right hand and align the fingers with the positive x-axis, then curl the fingers so they point in the direction of the positive y-axis, our thumb points in the direction of the positive z-axis.

![STATICS-CVF01%2001%2005%20b5097495074741e494f523137ace8694/right-hand_rule.png](STATICS-CVF01%2001%2005%20b5097495074741e494f523137ace8694/right-hand_rule.png)

b. Correct. This is the top view of the x-y plane, and the third axis, the z-axis, should come out of the page according to the right-hand rule.
