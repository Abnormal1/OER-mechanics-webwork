# Question:

Are the two couples below equivalent?

- [ ]  Yes
- [ ]  No

![STATICS-CMC05%2004%2003%2034f0fa4400b24018bb78ea0085b7601f/Untitled.png](STATICS-CMC05%2004%2003%2034f0fa4400b24018bb78ea0085b7601f/Untitled.png)

# Answer:

No

# Feedback:

If two couples produce a moment with the same magnitude and direction, then these two couples are equivalent. In this question, the couple on the left hand side produces a moment with a magnitude of $|\overrightarrow{M}|=50~\text{lb}~(2 ~\text{ft})= 100~\text{lb}\cdot\text{ft}$ and a CW rotational sense. However, the couple on the right hand side produces a moment with a magnitude of $|\overrightarrow{M}|=20~\text{lb}~(4 ~\text{ft})= 80~\text{lb}\cdot\text{ft}$ and CCW rotational sense. Thus, the two couples presented in this question are **NOT** equivalent.
